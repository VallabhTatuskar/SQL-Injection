About Data Collection

What Data We Don't Collect

  • We do NOT store your email addresses
  • We do NOT keep search history or logs
  • We do NOT share your data with third parties
  • We do NOT use cookies to track you

What Data We Use

  • Email hashes are processed in real-time only
  • We use the Have I Been Pwned database for breach checking
  • Temporary processing data is discarded immediately after checking

About Have I Been Pwned

We use the Have I Been Pwned (HIBP) API to check if emails have been compromised in known data breaches. HIBP is a legitimate service maintained by security researcher Troy Hunt that:

  • Provides anonymized breach data
  • Protects user privacy with k-anonymity
  • Is trusted by millions of users worldwide

Your Privacy Rights

By using this service, you acknowledge that your email will be checked against known breaches. However, no personal data is retained on our servers after the check is complete.
