Understanding the Process

Step 1: Hash Your Email

When you submit your email, it's hashed using SHA-1. Your actual email is never sent to our servers.

Step 2: Query Pwned Passwords API

We use the Have I Been Pwned API to check if your email hash exists in known breaches. The API only receives the first 5 characters of the hash (k-anonymity).

Step 3: Secure Comparison

We compare the remaining characters locally without exposing your full email hash to anyone.

Step 4: Results

You get instant results about whether your email has been found in any data breaches.

Privacy Note: We prioritize your privacy. Your email is never stored on our servers, and we don't keep logs of searches.
